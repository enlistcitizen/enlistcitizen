Test Before Upgrading

When you register for Star Citizen or Squadron 42 using our star citizen referral code, we’ll spawn for you or invite you to test whatever flyable ship in the verse, so you can be absolutely sure before you buy your dream ship. Being a Star Citizen backer can become an expensive hobby during the crowd funding phase, so testing before you pledge is a smart way to go. You can always grind in-game currency but this will of course be more time consuming. So for in-game purchasing as well, it’s a good idea to test drive first. And we’re here for you!

Level Up Your Earning Power

Want to rent a ship for a day but low on credit or can’t afford the current in-game market rate? Don’t worry! If a referral member, we got you! When available we’ll hook you up and you can cover the in-game tab later. If you’re not a referral member we’ll still rent out LTI ships to you at our discretion but an in-game rental fee will have to be gathered up front. But either way we’ll do what we can to be competitive! As appreciation for using our Star Citizen Referral Code STAR-GN2F-6JLW. Thank you!

The Support You Need

As a referral crew member, either a prospect or recruit, we will help you when you’re in a jam, for the most part and when available. We will what we can to help you get out of situations such as when pirates are pinning you down, or trouble makers are chasing you across the verse. We have all got each others back, and we’re here to help each other. Just be sure to not be too much of a trouble maker yourself, otherwise we might not intervene. We may also be able to help you earn reputation in-game with other factions such as alien races.
