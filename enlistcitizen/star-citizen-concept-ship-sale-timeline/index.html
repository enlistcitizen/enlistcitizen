Star Citizen Concept Ship Sales Timeline

This page is a timeline of all Star Citizen concept ship sales in order.
If you are new to Star Citizen or a veteran, this page may work well as a crash course in all Star Citizen spaceships or as a refresher.

Ship Concept Sales Timeline

2013
Saturday May 4th, 2013

RSI Auora CL, ES, MR, LN, and LX

The Aurora is a light single-crew spacecraft manufactured by Roberts Space Industries (RSI). It is marketed as an entry-level spacecraft with a lower price point making it one of the more common vehicles found in the United Empire of Earth (UEE). Several Aurora variants have been produced.

Friday June 21st, 2013

Origin 300i, 315p, 325a, and 350r

The premier luxury spaceflight experience is now available with extensive customization options with the Origin 300i series.

Saturday June 29th, 2013

Aegis Idris

The Aegis Idris is a medium-sized capital ship capable of storing smaller fighters and being run by a team of players working together.

Saturday September 21st, 2013

Drake Caterpillar

The Caterpillar is a multi-crew utility spacecraft and armored freighter manufactured by Drake Interplanetary.

Sunday October 13th, 2013

Aegis Avenger

The Aegis Avenger is a multi-role fighter sold by Aegis Dynamics.

Monday October 28th, 2013

Anvil Hornet F7A, F7C, F7C-R, F7C-S, F7C-M

The Anvil Hornet is the UEE Navy's premier carrier-based fighter craft and front-line attack ship for military combat missions.

Monday November 18th, 2013

Origin M50

The M50 is a single-seat performance racing spacecraft sold by Origin Jumpworks.

Friday November 22nd, 2013

Anvil Gladiator

The Gladiator is a two-seat light bomber developed by Anvil Aerospace.

Friday November 22nd, 2013

Aegis Retaliator

The Retaliator is a strategic bomber developed by Aegis Dynamics for the United Empire of Earth Navy.

Monday November 25th, 2013

Banu Merchantman

The Merchantman is a freighter commonly seen in the Banu Protectorate.

Monday November 25th, 2013

MISC Starfarer

The Starfarer is a tanker developed by Musashi Industrial Starflight Concern (MISC).

2014
Saturday June 7th, 2014

Freelancer DUR, MAX, and MIS

The Freelancer is a multi-role transport spacecraft developed by MISC and was the first vessel to be constructed as part of a unique lend-lease agreement with the Xi'an government.

Friday June 20th, 2014

Aopoa Khartu-Al

The Khartu-Al (Kal’tuāl) is a Xi’an light scout spacecraft adapted for Human use by Aopoa.

Friday July 11th, 2014

Aegis Gladius

The Gladius is a long-serving military light fighter spacecraft developed by Aegis Dynamics.

Friday August 15th, 2014

RSI Constellation Andromeda, Aquila, Pheonix, and Taurus

The RSI Constellation Andromeda is a multi-person freighter and the most popular ship in RSI's current production array.

Friday September 26th, 2014

Aegis Reclaimer

The Reclaimer is a specialized spacecraft manufactured by Aegis Dynamics.

Friday October 10th, 2014

Origin 85X

The 85X is a touring vessel manufactured by Origin Jumpworks.

Friday October 10th, 2014

Origin 890 Jump

The 890 Jump is a luxury civilian spacecraft built by Origin Jumpworks.

Friday October 31st, 2014

Drake Herald

The Herald is a data runner spacecraft sold by Drake Interplanetary.

Monday November 3rd, 2014

Aegis Redeemer

The Redeemer is a combination dropship-gunship developed by Aegis Dynamics for the United Empire of Earth Navy (UEEN).

Tuesday November 11th, 2014

Drake Cutlass Black, Red, and Blue

The Drake Cutlass variants are multiple role utility ships that range from fighting to search and rescue to bounty hunting.

Friday November 21st, 2014

Aegis Javelin

The Aegis Javelin is a massive, modular capital ship that can be appropriated for entrepreneurial use.

Friday November 28th, 2014

Anvil Carrack

The Carrack is a long-term exploration spacecraft developed by Anvil Aerospace for the United Empire of Earth Navy (UEEN).

Saturday December 20th, 2014

CNOU Mustang Alpha, Beta, Delta, Gamma

Four Consolidated Outland Mustang variants were offered, The CNOU Mustang Alpha, Mustang Beta, Mustang Delta, and Mustang Gamma.

2015
Sunday February 1st, 2015

RSI Orion

The Orion is a mobile mining platform manufactured by Roberts Space Industries (RSI).

Friday March 27th, 2015

Aegis Vanguard

The Vanguard Warden is a deep space fighter created by Aegis Dynamics for the United Empire of Earth Navy (UEEN).

Saturday April 25th, 2015

MISC Hull A, B, C, D, E

The MISC Hull series conquers various levels of cargo hauling through variants A, B, C, D, and E.

Friday May 22nd, 2015

MISC Reliant Kore

The Reliant is a light freighter developed by Musashi Industrial and Starflight Concern (MISC) as a versatile and agile entry to the intro-level ship market.

Saturday June 27th, 2015

Crusader Genesis Starliner

A Crusader Genesis Starliner is a type of commercial spacecraft used primarily for the transportation of passengers.

Friday July 24th, 2015

Kruger Merlin

The P-52 Merlin is an ultralight snub fighter spacecraft developed by Kruger Intergalactic.

Friday August 7th, 2015

Esperia Glaive

The Esperia Glaive is a reproduction of the Vanduul ship notable for its double-bladed wings, in contrast to the Scythe’s single-bladed wing, as well as the inclusion of a missile rack and a neutron gun.

Friday August 28th, 2015

Aegis Vanguard Warden, Harbinger, Sentinel

Aegis Vanguards have seen extensive service against the Vanduul.

Wednesday September 30th, 2015

MISC Endeavor

The Endeavor is a research spacecraft platform developed by MISC with a modular back end designed to be adapted to multiple roles.

Saturday October 10th, 2015

Aegis Sabre

The Sabre is a space superiority fighter sold by Aegis Dynamics

Thursday November 19th, 2015

Anvil Crucible

The Crucible is a repair spacecraft developed by Anvil Aerospace for the United Empire of Earth Navy (UEEN)

2016
Friday March 18th, 2016

Esperia Blade

The “Blade” is the official United Empire of EarthNavy (UEEN) designation for a standard light fighter operated by the Vanduul.

Friday April 22nd, 2016

MISC Prospector

The Prospector is a mining spacecraft developed by Musashi Industrial & Starflight Concern (MISC)

Friday May 27th, 2016

Drake Buccaneer

The Buccaneer is a civilian medium fighter sold by Drake Interplanetary.

Saturday June 18th, 2016

Drake Dragonfly

The Dragonfly is a personal open-canopy space and ground vehicle sold by Drake Interplanetary.

Friday August 19th, 2016

Anvil Terrapin

The Terrapin is a deep-space armored scout ship developed by Anvil Aerospace.

Thursday August 25th, 2016

Argo MPUV

The ARGO Astronautics MPUV-1P and MPUV-1C are dedicated transfer ships.

Monday October 10th, 2016

RSI Polaris

The Polaris is a corvette-class capital ship developed by Roberts Space Industries.

Friday November 18th, 2016

Esperia Prowler

The Prowler is the perfect fusion of two cultures: the elegance and effectiveness of the Tevarin war machine combined with the reliability of modern Human technology.

Friday December 2nd, 2016

MISC Razor

The Razor is a racing spacecraft developed by Musashi Industrial and Starflight Concern (MISC).

2017
Friday February 24th, 2017

Anvil Hurricane

The Hurricane is a strike fighter manufactured by Anvil Aerospace.

Saturday April 22nd, 2017

Banu Defender

The Banu Defender is a multi-crew fighter whose patchwork design highlights technology from a variety of species.

Friday May 19th, 2017

Aegis Eclipse

The Eclipse is a strategic bomber developed in 2930 by Aegis Dynamics.

Friday June 23rd, 2017

Aopoa Nox

The Nox is an open-canopy racer developed by Xi’an manufacturer Aopoa for Human use.

Friday July 14th, 2017

Tumbril Cyclone

The Cyclone is a light-armored ground vehicle developed by Tumbril Land Systems.

Friday August 25th, 2017

Origin 600i

The 600i series is a multi-role luxury vessel from Origin Jumpworks.

Friday September 29th, 2017

Origin X1

The X1 is an open-canopy snub spacecraft manufactured by Origin Jumpworks. Variants X1 Force and X1 Velocity fill out the X1 series from the X1 base model.

Friday October 27th, 2017

CNOU Pioneer

The Pioneer is a colonization and factory spacecraft sold by Consolidated Outland.

Friday October 27th, 2017

Aegis Sabre Raven

The Aegis Sabre Raven variant maintains all the speed and maneuverability of its Sabre forebear, but with a lower ship signature, making it a fast, stealthy infiltrator with EMP.

Friday November 24th, 2017

Anvil Hawk

More concepts will be added to this timeline as they are released.
