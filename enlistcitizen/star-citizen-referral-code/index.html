WHAT IS STAR CITIZEN?

Star Citizen is the groundbreaking and record-setting space simulator massively multiplayer online video game by Chris Roberts and Cloud Imperium Games. Chris Roberts is the acclaimed creator of the Wing Commander and Freelancer video games. Star Citizen aims to create a living, breathing science fiction universe with unparalleled immersion, and it is the largest crowdfunded project in history, having raised over 325 million USD to date.

The singleplayer version called Squadron 42 has Mark Hamill (Luke Skywalker) as your squadron leader. Squadron 42 has many many more science fiction fan-favorite Academy Award and Emmy and Golden Globe winning actors. Such as Gary Oldman (from movie franchises like Harry Potter and Batman), Henry Cavill (Witcher on Netflix and Superman), Mark Strong (Shazam, John Carter, and The Dark Crystal on Netflix), John Rhys-Davies (Indiana Jones and Lord of the Rings), Liam Cunningham (Game of Thrones), Ben Mendelsohn (Talos in Captain Marvel), Gillian Anderson (X-files), Sophie Wu (Kick-ass), and many more!

Test Before Upgrading

When you register for Star Citizen or Squadron 42 using our star citizen referral code, we’ll spawn for you or invite you to test whatever flyable ship in the verse, so you can be sure before you buy your dream ship. Being a Star Citizen backer can become an expensive hobby during the crowdfunding phase, so testing before you pledge is a smart way to go. You can always grind in-game currency, but this will, of course, be more time-consuming. So for in-game purchasing as well, it’s a good idea to test drive first. And we’re here for you!

Level Up Your Earning Power

Want to rent a ship for a day but low on credit or can’t afford the current in-game market rate? Don’t worry! If a referral member, we got you! When available, we’ll hook you up, and you can cover the in-game tab later. If you’re not a referral member, we’ll still rent out LTI ships to you at our discretion, but an in-game rental fee will have to be gathered upfront. But either way, we’ll do what we can to be competitive! As an appreciation for using our Star Citizen Referral Code STAR-GN2F-6JLW. Thank you!

The Support You Need

As a referral crew member, either a prospect or recruit, we will help you when you’re in a jam, for the most part, and when available. We will what we can to help you get out of situations such as when pirates are pinning you down or trouble makers are chasing you across the verse. We have all got each other’s back, and we’re here to help each other. Just be sure not to be too much of a trouble maker yourself. Otherwise, we might not intervene. We may also help you earn a reputation in-game with other factions such as alien races.
